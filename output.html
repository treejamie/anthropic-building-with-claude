# Marathon Nutrition Plan

## Daily Caloric Needs
- **Base metabolic rate**: ~1,700-1,800 kcal
- **With marathon training**: 2,800-3,500+ kcal/day (depending on training intensity)

## Macronutrient Breakdown

### Carbohydrates (55-65% of calories)
**Most important for marathon prep**
- 400-450g daily during heavy training
- Focus on: oats, rice, pasta, bread, potatoes, fruits, quinoa
- Time carbs around workouts for optimal absorption

### Protein (1.2-1.6g per kg body weight)
- 85-115g daily
- Sources: chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, legumes, lean beef

### Fats (20-30% of calories)
- 60-85g daily
- Sources: olive oil, nuts, avocado, fatty fish (omega-3s)

## Meal Timing Strategy

| When | What |
|------|------|
| **Pre-run** (2-3 hrs before) | Oatmeal + banana + honey |
| **During long runs** (90+ min) | Sports drink, gels, energy bars |
| **Post-run** (30-60 min) | Pasta + chicken + vegetables |
| **Daily snacks** | Fruit, yogurt, nuts, rice cakes |

## Key Recommendations
✓ Hydrate consistently (3-4L water daily, more on training days)
✓ Practice race-day nutrition during long training runs
✓ Increase carbs 3 days before race (carb-loading)
✓ Maintain consistent sleep and recovery nutrition

# Nutrition Plan for Boxing & Weight Loss

## Reality Check
Losing 3 kg in 2 weeks is **aggressive and risky** for an athlete. This is ~1.5 kg/week, which risks:
- Muscle loss
- Dehydration
- Performance decline
- Injury risk

**Better goal:** 1-1.5 kg/week (2-3 kg over 2 weeks) with proper hydration.

---

## Daily Calorie Target
- **Maintenance:** ~2,400-2,600 cal (active boxer)
- **For weight loss:** 1,900-2,100 cal/day (moderate deficit)
- **Adjust based on training intensity**

---

## Meal Framework (Sample Day)

### Breakfast (~500 cal)
- Oatmeal with banana, almond butter, honey
- Or: Rice + eggs (3) + vegetables

### Mid-Morning Snack (~200 cal)

- Or: Rice cakes + coconut oil

### Lunch (~600 cal)
- Grilled chicken/fish (150g)
- Sweet potato or white rice (150g)
- Vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
- Olive oil for cooking
